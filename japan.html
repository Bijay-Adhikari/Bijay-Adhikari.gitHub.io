Why Choose Japan?

Japan is a unique country that combines traditional and modern elements. It has a rich cultural heritage, advanced technology, and a thriving economy. Studying in Japan offers students the opportunity to experience a unique and fascinating culture while gaining a world-class education. The country is also safe, clean, and welcoming to international students.

Education System in Japan

The Japanese education system is highly regarded and emphasizes a strong work ethic and discipline. Higher education in Japan is divided into two types of degrees, as you mentioned. Most universities offer undergraduate and graduate degrees in Japanese, while some universities offer courses in English. There are also Japanese language schools for international students who wish to learn Japanese.

Lifestyle and Cost of Living in Japan

Japan has a high standard of living, and the cost of living can be relatively high compared to other countries. However, the country is known for its safety, cleanliness, and efficient public transportation system. Students can experience a unique culture that blends the traditional and the modern. In general, the cost of living in Japan can vary depending on the location and lifestyle.

International Tuition Fees in Japan:

Here's a summary of international tuition fees for some common courses in diploma/foundation degree, undergraduate degree, graduate degree, and post-graduate degree in Japan:

  • Diploma/Foundation degree: 700,000-1,000,000 JPY per year
  • Undergraduate degree: 1,000,000-1,500,000 JPY per year
  • Graduate degree: 1,500,000-2,000,000 JPY per year
  • Post-graduate degree: 2,000,000-2,500,000 JPY per year

Note that these are estimated figures and can vary depending on the university and the course.

Study Japanese in Japan

Japanese language schools are designed for international students who want to learn Japanese before they start their academic studies in Japan. These schools offer different types of courses, ranging from short-term to long-term programs. The duration of the course can vary from 6 months to 2 years, depending on the level of Japanese language proficiency required for the university and course.

The courses are designed to improve students' Japanese language skills, including reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Some language schools also offer cultural programs that introduce students to Japanese culture and customs.

The cost of Japanese language courses can vary depending on the duration of the program and the school. On average, the cost of a one-year Japanese language course can range from 700,000 JPY to 1,200,000 JPY.

Before enrolling in a Japanese language school, students need to meet the school's admission requirements, which usually include a minimum level of Japanese language proficiency. They also need to apply for a student visa to study in Japan.

Overall, Japanese language schools provide an excellent opportunity for Nepali students to learn the language and immerse themselves in Japanese culture before starting their academic studies. It is important to research the different language schools and their programs to find the best fit for your needs and goals.
